About Hui Wu

I am a research scientist at MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab. Prior to that, I worked in the computer vision and multimedia group at IBM Research AI, My current research interest includes vision and language, neural-symbolic learning, multi-modal dialogs and computer vision for fashion. Prior to joining IBM, I received my PhD in Computer Science from UNC Charlotte, with a thesis focus on manifold learning based machine learning techniques applied to image set analysis problems.

Contact me: wuhu AT us.ibm.com
